Notices

This Exchange is a fork of EtherDelta, using it's original Smart Contract and Order Book.
The URL for CraftDelta is https://chaincraft.cc/craftdelta. Bookmark it once and use the bookmark for your own safety. The only official shortcut is https://craftdelta.co, and will always redirect you to chaincraft.cc/craftdelta.

In the wake of modern ÐApp Browsers coming to all devices, we've put heavy emphasis on a Mobile-optimised experience. CraftDelta overcomes the limitations of EtherDelta and plays nicely with both Tablets and Mobiles of all sizes. Additionally, essential parts of CraftDelta are cached on your device upon your first visit, allowing blazing fast access even on a poor mobile Internet connection.

Do not send your tokens directly to the smart contract, or they will be lost and unrecoverable. Use the Deposit form ("Balance" on left) to put your funds (ETH and Tokens) on the exchange. CraftDelta cannot help you with funds incorrectly deposited to the EtherDelta trading smart contract.
